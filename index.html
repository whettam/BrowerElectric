About

Brower Electric is a family owned and operated business first established in Iowa in 1950. We have been doing business in Missouri since 1978 from our Bevier, MO office.

Our owners and supervisors have numerous years of experience in all aspects of residential, commercial, and industrial electrical wiring design and layout.
